# Your Claude phone — what's set up, and how to use it

Hey Lisa. I signed into your Claude account on my computer and connected a few basics so you can stop staring at Copilot. Nothing here touches Comply365 systems — this is all your personal stuff, so you can actually *play* with Claude before you pitch it at work.

Here's what's live, and how to use it from the Claude app on your phone.

## What I turned on for you

- **Claude Desktop** — the proper app, installed on my machine, signed into your account. This is where connectors and skills get managed. You don't need to touch it.
- **Gmail connector** — Claude can read and draft emails from your personal Gmail when you ask. It does **not** send anything automatically. It drafts.
- **Google Drive connector** — Claude can search your Drive and read docs you point it at.
- **Google Calendar connector** — "what's on my calendar tomorrow", "reschedule X" (as a draft).
- **A couple of starter Skills** — small pre-made behaviors: weekly-digest, meeting-prep, expense-summary. They show up as options inside a chat.

## Open the app and try these, in order

Open **Claude** on your phone. You'll see a blank chat.

**1. Warm up — just talk to it.**
Type: *"Give me a 3-line summary of my last 5 unread emails and rank them by what actually matters."*
Hit send. It will ask for permission the first time to use the Gmail connector. Say yes.

**2. Ask it to draft a reply.**
Follow up with: *"Draft a polite reply to [pick one of the emails], keep it under 4 sentences, and don't be cringe."*
It drafts in the chat. Copy/paste it to Gmail yourself — it won't send.

**3. Use a Skill.**
Tap the **+** icon in the compose area and pick **meeting-prep**. Paste a calendar invite or just say *"prep me for my 10am tomorrow."* It'll pull context and give you talking points.

**4. Ask about a doc in your Drive.**
*"Find the doc in my Drive called 'Q4 planning' and summarize it in 5 bullets."*

**5. Try Dispatch (if you want the next-level trick).**
In the iPhone app, tap the session switcher at the top. If I've left a Claude Code session running on my desktop, you can dispatch a task to it — e.g. *"clean up this CSV I'm sending you and email me the fixed version."* Your phone drives, my computer actually does the work. We'll use this later.

## What it *can't* do yet (and why)

- **You can't add new connectors from the phone.** The directory lives on desktop. If you want something new (Notion, Linear, whatever), message me on the `/lisa` page of claudeHub and I'll add it on my end.
- **You can't browse the Skills directory on mobile either.** Same deal — ask me.
- **Don't log in to Comply365 anything.** You know this already. Keep it personal until you've got internal sign-off.

## One habit that matters

When you ask Claude something, tell it **who the audience is** and **what format you want**.

Bad: *"Summarize this."*
Better: *"Summarize this for a Head of RevOps in 3 bullets, no jargon, and flag anything that looks like a deal-desk red flag."*

That one habit is 70% of what makes Claude feel smart vs. feel mid.
